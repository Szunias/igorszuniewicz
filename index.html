Introduction

A brief insight into who I am and my passion for audio and technology.

Hello! I am Igor Szuniewicz, a dedicated Composer, Audio Engineer, and Software Developer with a strong focus on creating immersive auditory experiences. My work spans technical sound design, the development of interactive music systems, and comprehensive game audio solutions. I thrive on blending creative artistry with technical precision to bring projects to life. This portfolio showcases my journey, skills, and the projects I am most proud of.
