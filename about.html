    About Me

    Composer, Audio Engineer, and Software Developer focused on interactive sound.

    I'm an audio engineer and composer who believes technology should enhance creativity, not complicate it. From real-time beatbox classification with 45ms latency to dynamic music systems in Unreal Engine, I build audio tools that actually work in production. Whether it's implementing adaptive soundscapes for games, developing custom VST plugins for 3D audio, or composing original scores for animations, I approach each project with both creative intuition and technical precision. My background spans classical piano training, mathematics and physics, and now game audio integration at DAE Belgium. I thrive on solving complex audio challenges and creating systems that respond intelligently to user interaction.

    My Journey

    My path into audio technology started with classical piano at age 6, where I learned discipline and musical structure. During high school, I discovered the mathematical beauty behind sound — how frequencies, harmonics, and digital signal processing could create entirely new sonic possibilities. This led me to develop machine learning systems for real-time audio analysis, create custom DSP algorithms for spatial audio, and build interactive music systems that adapt to player behavior. Now at DAE, I'm focusing on game audio integration, learning how to make audio systems that enhance gameplay and create emotional connections between players and virtual worlds.

    What Drives Me

    I believe great audio should feel magical to users while being robust under the hood. Every millisecond of latency matters when creating responsive systems. Every frequency band matters when crafting immersive soundscapes. I'm passionate about bridging the gap between artistic vision and technical implementation — creating tools that let composers and designers focus on creativity while the technology handles the complexity. Whether it's achieving 45ms latency for real-time beat detection or designing adaptive music that seamlessly responds to gameplay, I'm driven by the challenge of making the impossible feel effortless.
