Design Philosophy

01

Clarity First, Always

In game audio, readability trumps everything. I design from gameplay telemetry outward—ensuring critical feedback is never masked.

02

Technical Foundation

Understanding technical constraints—voice counts, memory budgets, CPU limits—enables better creative decisions.

03

Parametric Design

I approach sound design as modular layering: transients for impact, body for presence, texture for character.

04

Collaborative Iteration

Great audio comes from feedback loops with designers, programmers, and players. I document my work and iterate based on playtesting.
