🎮 Game Jam Project

Paw-ism

Execute your plans as an ambitious cat while avoiding being caught by the cleaning lady. A thrilling game jam project created in just 2 days for Unwrap Jam 2025.

🎮 Play on itch.io
2 Days Development Time
7 Team Members
Thriller Genre
The Concept

A Cat's Sinister Plan

Gather mice, navigate the roomba, and execute your plan before you're caught.

🐭

Collect Mice

Gather mice from the holes in the walls while staying alert to the cleaning lady's movements.

🤖

Use the Roomba

Jump on the roomba and use it as a tool or to move around more swiftly through the environment.

⚡

Execute Your Plan

Once everything aligns, there remains but only one thing to do... Complete your sinister objective.
