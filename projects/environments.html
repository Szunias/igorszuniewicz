About the Project

Modern Interior Design

This project showcases a modern interior design with realistic lighting and materials. Created in Blender 3D, the environment features detailed architectural elements, custom materials, and atmospheric lighting that brings the space to life. Every detail has been carefully crafted to create an immersive and photorealistic experience.

🔨 3D Modeling

  • Architectural layout and structure design
  • Detailed furniture and decoration modeling
  • Optimized topology for rendering efficiency
  • Precise measurements and scale accuracy

🎨 Material Design

  • Realistic PBR materials with proper roughness
  • Custom node setups for complex surfaces
  • Attention to detail in texture mapping
  • Accurate material properties and reflections

💡 Lighting & Atmosphere

  • Natural and artificial light balance
  • Volumetric lighting effects
  • Mood and atmosphere creation
  • Realistic shadow casting and global illumination
