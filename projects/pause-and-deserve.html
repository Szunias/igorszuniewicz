Pause & Deserve

First-person survival game with psychological horror elements and strategic pause mechanics.

Audio-Driven Horror Experience

Experience how audio design creates psychological horror: proximity-based threat sounds, reactive soundscapes, and dynamic tension-building through Wwise implementation.

Audio-Driven Horror Design

Pause & Deserve's horror atmosphere is built through immersive audio: proximity-based threat sounds intensify as Death approaches, environmental audio reacts to the pause mechanic, and layered soundscapes create relentless psychological tension in this endless survival challenge.

Horror Soundscape Design

Atmospheric horror built through layered sound design: ominous ambience, footstep-based positioning, and reactive audio systems create constant psychological tension without overwhelming gameplay clarity.

Oppressive Atmosphere

Layered drones, distant screams, and unsettling textures create constant unease

Proximity Warnings

Death's approach signaled through intensifying audio cues and heartbeat effects

Environmental Response

Nightmare landscape shifts reflected in audio: walls closing, echoes changing, lighting dimming

Reactive Audio System (Wwise)

Audio responds dynamically to gameplay: pause mechanic triggers time-warp effects, Death's speed influences sound intensity, player movement affects environmental reverb and spatial positioning.

Pause Mechanic Audio

Time-warp effect with pitch-down transitions and reverb tails during freeze moments

Adaptive Threat Level

Audio intensity scales with Death's proximity and speed, building unbearable tension

Spatial Audio Positioning

3D sound design helps players track Death's location through audio alone

Psychological Tension Techniques

Audio design creates relentless psychological pressure: subconscious anxiety through sub-bass frequencies, false audio cues that mislead players, and silence used strategically to heighten impact of sudden sound events.

Sub-Bass Anxiety

20-60Hz frequencies create subconscious dread and physical discomfort

Strategic Silence

Quiet moments amplify impact of sudden audio events, preventing desensitization

Audio Misdirection

False cues and spatial confusion add psychological gameplay layer

Audio Implementation Stack

Wwise Audio Middleware
Unreal Engine Integration

Real-time parameter-driven audio system with proximity detection, RTPC implementation, and dynamic mixing based on gameplay state.
