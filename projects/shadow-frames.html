The Concept

1960s Irish pub. Black and white. You have a Polaroid camera. Strange things happen. Sound tells you everything. If you mute the game, you can't play. That's the idea.

Audio Design Approach

Constant Tension

No jumpscares. Just dread building from drones and distant sounds you can't identify.

1960s Authentic

Polaroid clicks, vinyl crackle, tube radio static. Everything period accurate.

Sonic Uncertainty

Familiar sounds twisted just enough to make you question what you're hearing.

Anomaly System

Three audio stages guide investigation.

Stage 1

Alert: Something changed somewhere

Stage 2

Hint: Directional sound points you there

Stage 3

Presence: It waits until you capture it

Polaroid Mechanics

Your only defense. Also your biggest vulnerability.

Raise camera: tunnel hearing, lose awareness

Take photo: blind and deaf for 5 seconds

Reload film: can't move for 5 seconds

The Presence

Something's with you in the pub. Invisible. Audio only.

Footsteps that stop when you look

Irish Gaelic voices you can't understand

Sub-bass that grows with your mistakes
