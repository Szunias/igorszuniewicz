3D Audio Plugin Suite (VST Development)

Custom DSP for HRTF spatialization, real-time convolution, and UI design.

Overview

I developed a small suite of 3D audio plugins as a vehicle to deepen my DSP skills. The focus was on perceptual quality and CPU efficiency. I iterated on UI affordances to make complex processing discoverable and musical for everyday mixing.

Highlights

  • Custom HRTF interpolation and binaural rendering with diffusion
  • Partitioned FFT convolution with latency management
  • Responsive UI, sensible ranges, and parameter smoothing
