RAY ANIMATION MUSIC COMPOSITION

Complete musical composition for 3D animation project.

Replacement Composer Disco-inspired Ghent, Belgium Animation Score

Project Showcase

When the original composer from Ghent couldn't deliver, I stepped in to create the entire soundtrack from scratch. The result is a funky disco-inspired score that brings the animation to life with catchy melodies and vibrant energy.

  • Complete original score composition
  • Disco-inspired musical themes
  • Character-driven harmonic progression
  • Professional mixing and mastering

Project Details

3:30 Duration
100% Original
Disco Style
2024 Year

Bass & Rhythm Foundation

Funky disco bass line with tight drums - the groove that drives the whole track. Classic slap bass sound with rhythm guitar creating that authentic 70s dance floor energy.

Synths & String Layers

Shimmering retro synths and lush string arrangements. These layers add the sparkle and emotional depth - from bright analog leads to warm orchestral sweeps that build throughout the piece.

Complete Mix

The final stereo mix - all elements combined and mastered. Bass, drums, synths, strings and melodies working together to create that big, cinematic disco sound perfectly synced with the animation.
