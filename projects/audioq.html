Key Features

Professional Audio Processing

Everything you need for creative sound manipulation

🎵

Drag & Drop Loading

Seamlessly load audio files (WAV, AIFF, MP3) with intuitive drag-and-drop interface. Instant waveform visualization and playback control.

✨

Granular Synthesis

Advanced granular synthesis mode for textural manipulation. Control grain size, random playback, and crossfade transitions for unique sound design.

🌊

Custom Tremolo Effect

Manual DSP implementation with LFO calculation and per-sample amplitude modulation. Full control over rate and depth parameters.

🎚️

Filter System

High/Low-pass filtering with real-time parameter control. Frequency range from 20Hz to 20kHz for precise tonal shaping.

📊

Built-in Compressor

Professional compressor with full parameter automation. Control threshold, ratio, attack, and release for dynamic range management.

🔄

Advanced Looping

User-defined loop regions with tempo adjustment (20-300 BPM). Perfect for creating rhythmic patterns and sustained textures.

📈

Dual Waveform Display

Offline and real-time waveform visualization. Monitor your audio processing with professional-grade visual feedback.

🛡️

Volume Safety System

Built-in peak protection with automatic detection. Prevents audio damage with smart volume monitoring and safety alerts.

⚙️

Full Automation

AudioProcessorValueTreeState for complete parameter automation. All controls can be automated in your DAW for dynamic performances.
